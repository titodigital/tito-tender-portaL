🏢 About TITO Digital
Company background used to score, qualify, and pre-fill every proposal

TITO Digital Transformation Projects (Pty) Ltd

A South African ICT and digital transformation company based in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, directed by Gift Nyiko Nkuna. TITO specialises in cybersecurity, information security management, and AI-enabled digital transformation for government and enterprise clients.

ISO 27001 (SABS) ISO 9001 (SABS) Microsoft 365 Business Premium Reg: 2023/189029/07
📜 Compliance & Governance

TITO operates a validated ISO 27001 ISMS Manual (TITO-ISMS-MAN-001) and ISO 9001 QMS Manual (TITO-QMS-MAN-001), both effective 01 July 2025 and approved by Director Gift Nyiko Nkuna. The ISMS covers all 14 ISO 27001 clauses, Annex A (93 controls across four themes), a Microsoft 365-mapped risk register, Statement of Applicability, and a 15-folder SharePoint ISMS structure. The QMS covers all ten ISO 9001:2015 clauses, with CAPA managed via Power Apps and Planner, and customer satisfaction tracked through Microsoft Forms.

🧩 Platform & Tooling

Microsoft 365 Business Premium is TITO's primary ISMS and QMS platform — covering document control, HR security, supplier management, audit scheduling, and incident management (cross-referenced to TITO-SEC-POL-001). Claude AI (Anthropic) is integrated as an audit-assistance and policy-drafting tool across the compliance programme, including this Tender Intelligence Portal.
